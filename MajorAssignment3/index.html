My Experience as a UCF Student

My name is Joseleen Figueroa, and I am a transfer student from Valencia. I originally graduated with my AA in Business, and picked up the interest
of going back to school for my BS in Web designing. I believe it was the best choice I've made, because my experience here has been fun.
I remember just the other day I was introduced to coding and I thought to myself, there was no way I would learn this stuff, and now I just sit
amazed at what I can make. Right belowe this, I decided to make a list of the few reasons I enjoy my journey at UCF.

  • Learning
  • Making Friends
  • Learning new Codes
  • Staying Active
  • Creating
